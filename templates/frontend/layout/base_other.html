What are cookies?

Cookies are small text databases (files) that are usually downloaded to your computer, tablet or mobile device (hereinafter: device) from Internet sites which you visit. Cookies are used to operate all functions of the website, achieving a better user experience, and also enable the analysis of visits pages and advanced online advertising.

Types of cookies

To better understand cookies and how to control them, you can find out below what types of cookies exist, as well as how they are used on the website:

  • First-person cookies (first party cookies)Cookies that are on your device installed by the organization whose website you are visiting are known as first person cookies.
  • Third party cookies (engl. „third party cookies“)Cookies that are on your device installed by another organization in relation to the page you are visiting celebrities are like "third party" cookies.
  • Permanent cookies (engl. „persistent cookies“)Persistent cookies remain on Your device even after closing the browser. With the help of these cookies Websites store information to make it easier for you to use it. On for example, websites that require a username and password will remember your entry that will appear with each new visit.
  • Temporary cookies (engl. „session cookies“)Temporary cookies are removed with Your device at the time of closing the browser through which you are visited a particular website. These cookies store pages temporary data that serve for its proper functioning.
